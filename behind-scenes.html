Behind the Scenes

A peek into my creative process, daily inspirations, and the moments that shape my work

While my public work focuses on education and creativity, there's so much more that happens behind the scenes. This page gives you a glimpse into my creative process, the challenges I face, and the small moments that inspire my work.

Preparing for Workshops

Hours of preparation go into every workshop. From testing equipment to creating step-by-step guides, I ensure everything runs smoothly for participants.

Fun fact: I always arrive 2 hours early to set up and test everything, even though it usually only takes 30 minutes. Better safe than sorry!

My Creative Process

Creativity doesn't always happen in a straight line. My process involves lots of brainstorming, experimentation, and sometimes starting over completely.

I keep a "failure journal" where I document what didn't work and why. It's become one of my most valuable resources for learning and growth.

Daily Inspiration

Inspiration can come from anywhere. I find ideas in nature, conversations with students, books, and even random YouTube videos.

I maintain a digital "inspiration board" where I collect ideas, quotes, and images that spark my creativity. It's my go-to when I need fresh perspectives.

Work-Life Balance

Finding balance between my passion for education and personal time is an ongoing journey. I'm learning to set boundaries and take breaks.

My favorite way to recharge? Taking long walks with my camera, capturing the beauty of everyday moments.

"The best ideas often come when you're not actively looking for them. Give your mind space to wander."
