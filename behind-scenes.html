Field Notes: Community Workshop

Date: April 5, 2023

Location: Community Center

Participants: 15 students, ages 10-14

Observations:

  • Students were initially hesitant but became more engaged as they saw others succeeding
  • The hands-on approach really helped with understanding abstract concepts
  • Pairing students with different skill levels worked well for peer learning

Next Steps: Consider adding more visual aids and possibly extending the workshop duration
