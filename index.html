About Me

As an educator and STEAM advocate, I believe in the power of hands-on learning and creative problem-solving. My journey in education began with a simple question: "How can we make learning more engaging and meaningful?"

Over the years, I've developed programs that combine technology, art, and critical thinking to help students discover their potential. From robotics workshops to digital storytelling projects, I create spaces where curiosity thrives and innovation happens.

When I'm not in the classroom, you might find me:

  • Volunteering with youth programs and community events
  • Running hands-on robotics and tech workshops
  • Sketching random ideas in my notebook
  • Taking photos of everyday moments
  • Getting lost on a hiking trail or planning the next one
  • Dancing (badly but happily)
